Our Story

More Than a Salon.
It's Family.

What started as Claudia's passion for hair and beauty has grown into one of Richardson's most beloved salon and barbershop destinations. For over 15 years, clients have trusted us with their most personal transformations — and many have become lifelong friends.

We're a true family business. Claudia leads the salon side while her husband Wildo runs the barbershop — together with their talented team, they serve men, women, and children of all hair types and backgrounds.
